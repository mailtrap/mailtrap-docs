## Analytics Features

### [Statistics](../statistics.md)
Detailed performance metrics for all your email marketing activities. Track opens, clicks, conversions, and more.

## Key Metrics

### Engagement Metrics
- **Open Rate**: Percentage of recipients who opened
- **Click-Through Rate (CTR)**: Percentage who clicked links
- **Click-to-Open Rate (CTOR)**: Clicks relative to opens
- **Conversion Rate**: Percentage who completed desired action
- **Forward Rate**: Percentage who shared your email

### Delivery Metrics
- **Delivery Rate**: Successfully delivered emails
- **Bounce Rate**: Emails that couldn't be delivered
- **Spam Complaints**: Recipients marking as spam
- **Unsubscribe Rate**: Recipients opting out
- **List Growth Rate**: Net subscriber change

### Revenue Metrics
- **Revenue per Email**: Total revenue / Emails sent
- **Revenue per Subscriber**: Total revenue / Subscribers
- **ROI**: (Revenue - Cost) / Cost × 100
- **Customer Lifetime Value**: Predicted total value
- **Average Order Value**: From email campaigns
