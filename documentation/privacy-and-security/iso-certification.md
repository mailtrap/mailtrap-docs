# ISO Certification

Mailtrap is proud to be ISO/IEC 27001:2022 certified, demonstrating our commitment to the highest standards of information security.

In April 2024, we have successfully completed the ISO certification process, meeting all requirements for ISO/IEC 27001:2022 and officially achieving certification.

In March 2025, we have underwent a surveillance audit that confirmed our ongoing compliance, allowing us to maintain our certification without interruption.

This certification demonstrates that Mailtrap adheres to rigorous security practices for managing information security risks, safeguarding data, and continually enhancing our security controls.
